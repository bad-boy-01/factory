"Hey, little brother, are the things you're carrying eggs?" Xu Dagui asked when he saw the eggs in Xu Changshou's hand.
"Ah, Sister Liu found our amber eggs so delicious that she ordered twenty more. These twenty eggs are the eggs she brought, when the time comes we can get three copper coins for each," Xu Changshou said.
"Hmm, this way we won't have to buy them. Selling them will also earn us sixty more copper coins. Little Brother, you're really great." Xu Dagui's happiness increased even more; the more the family earned, the sooner he could bring his wife back home.
"Let's go, let's buy more eggs and salt, let's make more," Xu Kaixi said.
When they returned to their homes in Xiaoyan village, it was starting to get dark. Family members locked themselves in the room and had a family meeting among themselves for that day.
"Looking at today's amber egg sales, this business is sustainable." As the head of the family, Xu Kaixi took the first word.
As soon as he finished speaking, a cheerful smile appeared on everyone's faces. The elder Xu family had finally found a way to earn money; but it hadn't been easy at all.
"Changshou is the most impressive one. If it weren't for Changshou, we wouldn't have been able to sell everything today, let alone get a big order of twenty amber eggs." Then Xu Dagui, taking the word, pointed out the person who made the biggest contribution to the trip.
"Father, mother, today we saw that most ordinary people cannot afford our amber eggs. This is not a long-term market; we must continue to expand towards the rich market." Xu Changshou spoke last, touching directly upon the essence of the business.
"We are all farmers; how can we enter the market of the rich?" The family began to worry again.
"Restaurants. We can cooperate with restaurants. As long as they are willing to buy our amber eggs, we don't need to worry about sales," Xu Changshou said.
"Right! Rich people eat in restaurants. If we sell to restaurants, they will naturally recognize our amber eggs and thus the market will open. Changshou, where do you know all this from?" Xu Dagui was the first to react and shouted excitedly.
"Then, after producing this batch of amber eggs, we will go to every restaurant to promote them. As long as we secure one or two, we won't have to worry about sales," Xu Kaixi made his final decision.
The group, not wanting to waste any more time, worked overnight to turn the eggs they bought that day into amber and placed them in jars. Seven days later, fifty amber eggs were taken out of the jars. Everyone had already got used to the fact that the rice in the rice jar didn't decrease much. As a result, the Elder Xu Family was able to eat one meal consisting of solid food every day.
After breakfast Xu Kaixi took Xu Dagui and Xu Changshou back to town.
"Father, let me go and take the amber eggs Sister Liu wanted, wait," Xu Changshou said.
"Since she manages our affairs so well, we should go and pay our respects in person," Xu Kaixi said.
"Don't. She is a young lady; it wouldn't be appropriate for her to meet with male guests." Xu Changshou hurriedly stopped him. If he let them follow him, wouldn't the secret be revealed? It would also be hard for him to do anything in the future.
He ran away without waiting for Xu Kaixi's response.
"Sister Liu, here are the amber eggs you wanted." Xu Changshou gave the amber eggs to Cuier.
"Little Changshou, these amber eggs are really very good for making porridge. My father loves them; from now on bring me twenty every ten days," Liu Luoling said while cupping Xu Changshou's small face.
As a matter of fact, Liu Wen had come here to eat a few days ago and immediately fell in love with the taste of amber eggs. He had wanted to take some home to share with his colleagues and superiors.
Receiving such a long-term and large order, Xu Changshou behaved incredibly politely. After letting Liu Luoling play until she was satisfied, he left. In his wallet he now had the deposit amounting to fifty copper coins and the final payment amounting to fifty copper coins from the Liu family.
"I'm an old woman and I can't eat this much rice. If you give it to me for five copper coins a pound, will I help you sell it too?" When Xu Changshou put ten pounds of brown rice in front of the old woman, this time the woman didn't buy the rice immediately.
"Alright. This is ten pounds, fifty copper coins in total."
After starting the amber egg business, he no longer relied much on the money he obtained from brown rice. Since he was with Xu Kaixi and Xu Dagui, he couldn't stay away for long. Finding a middleman to help him sell the rice was the best solution for him.
"Good. If you have more rice in the future, come back to me. This old woman will have earned the price difference." Seeing Xu Changshou accept, the old woman beamed with joy, the wrinkles on her face becoming more prominent.
"Wait a bit, I'll be right back."
Xu Changshou took the money and ran out of the alley. He still had ten pounds of brown rice and forty-three eggs he bought in the storage. He had spent almost all the money he had saved earlier in the last few days to buy brown rice and eggs. He hadn't brought them all out at once because he was worried the old woman would wonder how a child could carry twenty pounds of rice and that she couldn't consume that much.
When the woman asked him to bring more, he decided to sell these ten pounds too. Seeing the woman bringing another large bag of rice that Xu Changshou didn't know where he brought from, the woman became suspicious, but didn't ask. She assumed the child's parents were nearby and therefore didn't dare deceive Xu Changshou.
With one hundred copper coins in hand, after deducting the rice and egg money, he had more than two hundred copper coins left. He had to give sixty copper coins to Xu Kaixi as payment for the amber eggs in return for Liu Luoling. This left him with one hundred and forty-two copper coins.
He spent twenty more copper coins to buy twenty eggs in the mall and walked towards Xu Kaixi with a total of sixty-three eggs including the forty-three eggs he had kept.
"Father, this is the final payment of sixty copper coins. Big Sister Liu told us to send twenty amber eggs every ten days. She sold us sixty eggs for only one hundred and ten copper coins, even gave three extra, and she even allows us to buy on credit," said Xu Changshou.
"This Ms. Liu must be raising chickens in her house; she has too many eggs," exclaimed Xu Dagui.
"Shall we go to restaurants and promote them?" Xu Changshou suggested, wanting to quickly interrupt Xu Dagui's words. However, he thought Liu Luoling's image while raising chickens should be very beautiful.
"Alright, business first." The three of them walked together towards Zuixian Restaurant.
Xu Kaixi, without entering into fancy talk, directly explained the method of making amber eggs. Then the restaurant's chef made a dish consisting of amber eggs with cold sauce and lean meat porridge prepared with amber eggs. The manager was very satisfied and agreed to supply ten eggs every day to Zuixian Restaurant.
The three of them didn't expect things to go so smoothly, so they set off towards Yuelai Restaurant. This time Xu Changshou stated that Zuixian Restaurant had already ordered and Yuelai Restaurant accepted the daily supply of ten eggs without any hesitation. There was no other reason; these were the only two restaurants in town and they were rivals. They had to have everything their rival had.
The trio went to several small taverns. When they said that both large restaurants had ordered amber eggs, two of the more ambitious taverns decided to buy a few to try. Things went more smoothly than they imagined.
This time they sold a total of forty-seven amber eggs and provided one hundred and ninety-five copper coins in income, providing a net profit of over ninety copper coins.
"Let's go home. We'll eat the remaining three eggs ourselves; we won't sell them," Xu Kaixi said loudly. He wanted to buy more eggs to take home, but Xu Changshou stopped him.
"Father, sister Liu said she could give us twenty eggs a day for only thirty copper coins from now on. Let's use the eggs we have first, and I'll buy more from her tomorrow," Xu Changshou said.
Since the eggs in the mall were cheap, there was no need to spend money by buying expensive eggs, and this gave him a perfect opportunity to go to town and back every day.
"Ah, Ms. Liu is really a good person. Others are selling them for two copper coins this year, but she only wants one and a half copper coins. That means she has quite a lot of chickens," exclaimed Xu Dagui.
"Yes, yes, she has been growing them for two and a half years," Xu Changshou replied weakly, not daring to say anything more. "Sorry, Sister Liu!" Xu Changshou could only apologize from his heart.
"After this, when we sell her amber eggs, we'll only take the cost price from her," said Xu Kaixi. Since she had done such a great favor to them, it was impossible for them to profit from her. Farmers had their own unique principles.
Thus Liu Luoling, without realizing it, became the egg supplier of the Elder Xu Family.
When they returned to Xiaoyan village, they put about two hundred copper coins on the table and the whole family was excited for a long time.
"Oh, we'll have money from now on!" "Ms. Liu's eggs are cheap. Now we can earn three copper coins per egg. Two restaurants need twenty eggs a day, which means six hundred copper coins a month, so we can earn—we can earn—" Xu Dagui was standing on the side, scratching his head, he couldn't calculate.
"One thousand eight hundred copper coins!" said Xu Changshou. "Right, one thousand eight hundred copper coins!" Xu Dagui clapped his hands excitedly.
"Also, if we do some sales here and there, we can earn two thousand copper coins a month. That makes two ounces of silver!" Xu Kaixi was also jumping for joy.
"Life will be better this year." Zhang Xiue wiped the tears from the corners of her eyes; after painful days, hope for a better life had finally dawned.
Xu Changshou also finally breathed a sigh of relief. He had finally found a way to earn money. By reselling the eggs he bought from the mall every day, he could earn ten copper coins, that is, three hundred copper coins a month. Also, by reselling brown rice every day, he could earn three copper coins per pound, that is, two hundred and seventy copper coins a month. With this money, it would be much easier to do other things.
Since the restaurants had agreed that the first batch of amber eggs would be delivered within eight days, the whole family started working to prepare amber eggs after dinner.
The next day Xu Changshou went to town taking Xu Youtian with him. Xu Kaixi gave them twenty copper coins and told them to buy one pound of pork to take with them. Now that the family had found a way to earn money, they shouldn't let the children suffer.
Xu Changshou found an excuse to secretly move away from Xu Youtian, bought a portion of fatty pork belly from his place and spent only ten copper coins. Then he entered Swallow Alley, found a corner, took out twenty eggs and finally met with Xu Youtian.
On today's dinner table there was an extra plate of scrambled eggs and a bowl of soft-boiled pork belly. Although the spices weren't very intense, they were delicious enough to make the children's mouths water. Looking at the meal, which was not very flashy but much better than those in the past, Zhang Xiue was moved once again, tears appeared in the corners of her eyes.
"Take this plate of food to your grandmother and grandfather." Zhang Xiue took some eggs and pork belly from the plate, filled more than half of the plate and handed it to Xu Youtian with instructions. As their families started to improve their meals, they naturally couldn't forget their elders.
"Okay!" Xu Youtian grabbed the bowl and ran away.
The Old Xu Family Residence was not very far from Xu Kaixi's family home, less than half a mile away. The Old Residence was home to Old Master Xu and his wife, as well as the families of the eldest son Xu Kaihai, the second son Xu Kaijiang and the fourth son Xu Kaiquan.
The Residence, although old and simple, was located on a large land.
"Is this You Tian?" Old Master Xu, Xu Shuli, asked as he saw Xu Youtian run inside while he was waiting for the meal in the courtyard, several women were working in the kitchen.
"Grandfather, this is what my mother wanted me to bring to honor you both." Xu Youtian put the bowl on the stone table in front of the old man.
"Meat? Eggs? Where did you find these?" Xu Shuli craned his neck, looked and exclaimed.
"We bought them from town," Xu Youtian replied honestly.
"Changshou has just recovered from his illness; how can you spend money like this? Take this meat back and let Changshou fill his stomach." Xu Shuli was reluctant to eat.
"There is more at home. I won't say anything else, I have to go back to the meal. Goodbye, grandpa." Xu Youtian, thinking of the pork breast at home, greeted him and quickly moved away.
"Old man, who was here?" Xu Kaixi's mother, Mrs. Xu Yang, heard the noise and came out of the kitchen, then several of her daughters-in-law followed her.
"You Tian came to bring us a bowl of meat and eggs."
"Meat? Eggs?" The women gathered around and started looking and immediately broke into a chorus of exclamations.
"Does the third branch of the family have money? How can they afford to eat meat?" said Xu Kaijiang's wife Zhao Caifeng.
"A few days ago Lai Shun and Lai Xi saw children from the third branch of the family picking wild vegetables on the mountain. I wonder where they get the money?" said Xu Kaihai's wife Madam Xu Wang, with a suspicious expression.
Even though she said she didn't believe it, rolling her eyes, she thought she should go and find out what was happening. How did the third branch suddenly become able to eat meat? At that time, fearing that Xu Changshou would pull them down, she had forced the third branch to divide the family and live separately. Now that they were living better than them, wasn't that a slap in her own face?
